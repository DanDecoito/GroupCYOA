Murder Mystery

He follows the clues leading back to his old highschool, where it all happened. When he gets there he finds that the killer has taken his wife and his two kids and is holding them hostage. He finds a ransome note that says if he doesnt make up for past things his family will be murdered. When the detective finds another clue he learns that the killer who has his family is the shy kid from his class that he used to bully every day for all of high school. The killer is now strong and smart enough to seek the perfect revenge, so the he took the detectives whole family and is waiting for him to set things straight.
